About Me 👩

Hi! I am Shriya, a UX Designer. I have a background in computer science and graphics design which gives me a unorthodox way to look at things that combine
aesthetics 🎨 + practical solutions 🔬.
I have a keen interest in transforming communities through design-informed solutions.

I completed my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering from University of Pune and then went on to get a Master’s degree in Human-Computer Interaction from University of Michigan, Ann Arbor (#GoBlue) where I worked on research projects that involved mitigating harassment on social media through design interventions.
